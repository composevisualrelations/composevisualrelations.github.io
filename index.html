Image Generation - Real World Block Dataset

Our proposed methods can be extended to real-world scenes. We used the real-world block dataset proposed in https://arxiv.org/pdf/1603.01312.pdf. We compare the image generation results of our proposed method, i.e. Ours (Learned Embedding) and Ours (Sentence CLIP), and the best baseline, i.e. StyleGAN2 (CLIP) on this dataset. The images are generated conditioned on a single relational description (1R), two relational descriptions (2R), and three relational descriptions (3R), respectively. Our approaches are able to generate images containing the mentioned object relations and outperform StyleGAN2 (CLIP).

Image Generation - Visual Genome

Our proposed methods can be extended to real-world scenes, such as visual genome. We train our model on a small subset of visual genome dataset by selecting the common relations and objects. We found that our proposed method with CLIP as the encoder, i.e. Ours (Sentence CLIP) performs better than our method with a learned embedding, i.e. Ours (Learned Embedding).

Input Relational Scene Description
Building next to sky
Building in front of sky
Grass underneath building
Tree below building
Grass to right of woman

Image Generation (Similar Objects) - CLEVR

We use Ours (Learned Embedding) to generate images given relational scene descriptions that only contain objects with the same attributes. Our model can generate multiple similar objects rather than one object, even if the relation descriptions do not mark the identities of these objects.

Input Relational Scene Description
A small metal cyan cube in front of a small metal cyan cub and, small metal cyan cube behind a small metal cyan cube
A small rubber gray cube left a small rubber gray cube, and a small rubber gray cube behind a small rubber gray cube
A small green metal cylinder below a small green metal cylinder, and a small green metal cylinder on the right of a small green metal cylinder
A large metal green sphere above a large metal green sphere, and a large metal green sphere on the left of a large metal green sphere
A small purple metal cylinder above a small purple metal cylinder, and a small purple metal cylinder below a small purple metal cylinder
A large red metal cube behind a large red metal cube, and a large red metal cube above a large red metal cube
A small yellow metal cylinder behind a small yellow metal cylinder, and a small yellow metal cylinder above a small yellow metal cylinder
